OUR HOSPITAL

Shraddha Polyclinic & Nursing Home is situated in Mulund West. Its inception was done on 26th January, 1996. With a vision to offer the best in patient care and equipped with technologically advanced healthcare facilities, they are one of the upcoming names in the healthcare industry in suburbs. A team of well-trained medical staff, non-medical staff and experienced clinical technicians work round-the-clock to offer various services

A team of doctors on board, including specialists, are equipped with the knowledge and expertise for handling various types of medical and surgical cases. It caters to various specialties like medicine, cardiology, surgery, orthopaedics, etc. It is a 37-bedded nursing home having well-equipped ICU with central monitoring station and OT with C-Arm. The hospital also has in-house ventilators, Dialysis machine, 2-D Echo and USG machine, Stress Test machine, computerized ECG reporting, etc. The hospital also provides free local ambulance service.
