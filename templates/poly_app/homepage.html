About Us

We are a Multispeciality clinic situated at prime location in Darwha city. PariJit holds pride in being able to deliver quality services to their patients. We are centres for multiple treatment services like General Medicine, Urology, Chest medicine and Dentistry are managed with highly qualified doctors. We genuinely care, because care and compassion is our culture. We believe compassion is as powerful as any medicine to cure mankind.
